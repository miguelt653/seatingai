Call Flow Logic

Our AI voice assistant follows a structured and friendly conversation flow to ensure reservations are booked accurately and callers feel well‑served. Here is a high‑level overview of the steps it takes:

  1. Greeting and Intent – The assistant answers with a warm greeting and asks if the caller needs a new reservation, a modification/cancellation, or general information.
  2. Collect Details – For new reservations it gathers party size, date, time and any special requests. For changes or cancellations it asks for the reservation name and existing date/time.
  3. Check Availability – It checks real‑time availability and offers alternative times if the requested slot is full.
  4. Confirmation – It confirms the details, collects contact information (name, phone or email) and summarizes the booking before logging it in the system.
  5. Wrap Up – Finally, it offers directions or menu information and asks if there is anything else before ending the call.
